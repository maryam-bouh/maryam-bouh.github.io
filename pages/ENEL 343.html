ENEL 343

ENEL 343 was another exciting class I took this semester! By performing hands-on labs and projects I was able to grasp many of the important concepts learned in this class. One particular lab that I took an interest in was building a speaker. We built and designed the speaker using a TLV4112 op-amp.

Some of the skills I gained from doing this lab is:
  • Reading and being able to use datasheets
  • Incorrect placement of wiring and or capacitors allows for inaccurate results
  • Working in a team provided beneficial results
  • Being able to communicate with you team helped aid in the results

This speaker circuit was very beneficial in our final project. We were able to incorporate speakers into the sound system of the alarm clock making it a prime feature. Making it with adjustable sound (using a potentiometer), and a silent feature which catered towards people that are hard of hearing.
